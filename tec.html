High-Pressure Steam

High-pressure steam carpet washing utilizes pressurized steam generated by a specialized machine with a high-pressure boiler. This heated steam is applied to the carpet, effectively loosening and removing dirt and stains from deep within the fibers. The high pressure ensures a thorough cleaning process, while the heat helps sanitize the carpet by eliminating bacteria and allergens. This method is environmentally friendly as it often requires minimal or no chemical detergents, making it an efficient and eco-conscious carpet cleaning solution.
